About Us

We are a recently established driving academy with fifteen years of experience. By ensuring that our students receive the best instruction, practice, and experience, we hope to make our roads safer for everyone and ultimately make secure drivers out of our students.

Wayne has always had a passion for cars, which inspired him to pursue a career as a driving instructor. He was aware that as time went on, the dangers on the roads kept increasing, and he wanted to help bring about a change in how people are taught. He is now engaging in a brand-new, exciting venture after years of experience. Now that he is the owner of Wayne's Driving Academy, he has set high standards for his students, ensuring that they would be distinguished from the rest through their driving knowledge and abilities. Our goal is for all of our students to pass on their first attempt and set a good example for other drivers for a risk-free driving experience.
